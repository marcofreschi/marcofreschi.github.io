Research Statement

Three Pillars
Materials Science Sustainability Design Eng.

Materials Science

Rigorous, data-driven investigation of functional materials across a broad spectrum of applications — from tribological composites to biodegradable morphing systems.

Design Engineering

Application-driven research guided by real-world requirements and inspired by natural mechanisms — translating biological strategies into engineered solutions.

Sustainability

Environmental, economic, and social dimensions integrated from the earliest design stage — not as an afterthought, but as a core design constraint.
